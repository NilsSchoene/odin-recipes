Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice dishes are popular in Asia and combine rice with different sorts of ingredients like vegetables, meat or meat alternatives. This fried rice recipe adds carrots, kohlrabi alongside with vegan ground beef into the mix for a quick vegan dish. Makes 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 400 g rice
  • 40 ml vegetable oil
  • 4-6 carrots
  • 250 g vegan ground beef
  • 1 kohlrabi
  • 12 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp garlic oil
  • 4 tbsp tomato paste
  • 4 tsp sambal oelek

Steps

  1. Peel the carrots and the kohlrabi. Cut the carrots in small pieces and slice the kohlrabi in thin slices.
  2. Cook the rice as described on the package instructions and set aside when finished.
  3. Meanwhile heat the vegetable oil in a big pan. Once heated add the carrots and cook them for about 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add the vegan ground beef to the pan and cook for another 3-4 minutes or until it gets a light brownish color.
  5. Add the sliced kohlrabi and cover the pan with a lid. Let it cook for another 4-5 minutes while stirring occasionally.
  6. Mix the other remaining ingredients in a large cup and add them to the pan along with the cooked rice. Let everything heat through and cover with a lid if necessary. Enjoy!
